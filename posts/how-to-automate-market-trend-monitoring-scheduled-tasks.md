# How to Automate Market Trend Monitoring with Scheduled Tasks

Most marketing teams check the same sources every week: competitor websites, ad libraries, Google Search Console, social feeds, review platforms. The information is useful. The process of collecting it manually is not.

The average marketing team spends 5-10 hours per week on recurring monitoring tasks that produce no creative output — just inputs for decisions. Scheduled task automation eliminates this entirely. You define what you want monitored, how often, and where you want the output delivered. The work runs without you.
